3. Specification of bg.mp4
    height: 1920p
    width: 1080p
4. To convert a 16:9 1080p video to a 9:16 use this command:
    ffmpeg -i background.mp4 -filter:v "crop=ih*9/16:ih,scale=1080:1920" -c:a copy output.mp4
